Zinus

Originally a white-label brand, Zinus was the first company to put “beds in a box,” and is now selling their supremely comfortable mattresses and beds direct to consumers for the first time.

Our goal was to help Zinus increaseing their awareness into new markets through organizing a pop-up event co-featured the movie Big's 30th year anniversary. We put Zinus' "bunk bed" and other toys in a NYC apartment to recreate Josh's bedroom from the original movie. I was the designer in team to help designing the media invite, social post and room renderings that are used for the event's campaign.

Deliverables: Branding / Pop-up Event / Social Campaign / 3D Rendering

Tool used: Adobe Illustrator / Adobe InDesign / Adobe Photoshop / Cinema 4D / Octane Render

Background

  • Design Problem - I've always want to be able to record the books that i read and continuously find more interesting books to read through a trusting system.

  • Goal - So i wanted to mostly focus on two things in this design: to record easily and to recommend more books based on the other people's reading list.

  • Insights - I find the most efficient recommend system for me is to give reading lists that contains the books that i've read and rated high. But not "similar" books that share the same topic or subject.

  • Features and solutions

    • recommend book - based on booklists.
    • better, faster, simpler mark system.

Background/ Design Problem

I've always want to be able to record the books that i read and continuously find more interesting books to read through a trusting system.

















































User Research

But as an average, I find it's really hard to find an app that always gets me the book i like.















































Goal

So to design this app, i wanted to mostly focus on two things: to record easily and to recommend more books based on the other people's reading list.






































Insights

I find the most efficient recommend system for me is to give reading lists that contains the books that i've read and rated high. But not "similar" books that share the same topic or subject.













































Brainstorms - features and solutions

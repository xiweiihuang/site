Background

  • Design Problem - Every designer seems have designed a player app or two, and i'am no exception.

  • Goal - This design focuses on a clean, slate style in design and interaction.

  • Insights - I came up with a design of card forms that show albums and artist info that will pop up from the bottom of the screen. In my opinion, all the necessary interactions one have to perform when using music player is to be able to change songs, albums and perhaps playlist, look after artists, album and song info. I think it's ideal all this essential controls can be done using one hands within a seamless and constant interaction fashion.

  • Features and solutions

    • Main playing
    • A saved song list
    • Lyric
    • All songs
    • Album collection
    • Artist - all albums
    • Artist - one album - one song

Background/ Design Problem

Every designer seems have designed a player app at some point, and i'am no exception.

















































User Research















































Goal

This design focuses on a clean, slate style in design and interaction.






































Insights

Albums and artist info presented in card forms show at the bottom of the screen. In my opinion, all the necessary interactions one have to preform when using music player is to be able to change songs, albums and perhaps playlist, look after artists, album and song info. I think it's ideal all this essential controls can be done using one hands within a seamless and constant interaction fashion.

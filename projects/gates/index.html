Global Findex Data Animation Storyboard

1.7 Billion of people lack access to formal banking, which keeps them stuck in poverty. Mobile money (simple digital wallets that transfer funds a lot like sending and receiving text msgs) is reaching more and more of these people every day. The World Bank (with funding from the client) has released data from 2014 and 2017 taking a general pulse of who is and isn't banked, broken down by geography and a thousand other factors. We're most interested in mobile account ownership overall, among women, and among rural communities in select countries in Africa.

Our assignment is to bring Global Findex data to life through animated infographics. I worked closely with the art director and animator to create all the visual assets and typographical elements based on the project's overarching concept.

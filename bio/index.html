Xiwei







Hi, my name is Xiwei Huang. Wuhan born and bred. Product of the late-2000s MP3/4 generation. Glass half-full kind of gal. Recently a media art master graduate from NYU. Before that I spent my days and nights studying theories in mass communication and researching on cognitive psychology at UCB.

Mostly working for creating better experiences we have with today's fancy technology, also spent some time building kinetic installations and designing 3D content.

Enjoying the life being a habitual observer, drinking way too many coffee to stay sane. Thrived in lots of failures and some accomplishments. Open to big ideas and surprising endeavors.

Talk to me if you feel the same way as I do.
